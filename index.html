Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 9, 2026

At CHADMAX AI, we respect your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle "Face Data" and personal information to ensure total transparency.

01.

Collection of Face Data

When you use our "AI Scan", we collect facial geometry data (landmarks, symmetry proportions, and skin texture) from the photos you upload. This is strictly "Face Data" for analysis.

02.

Purpose of Processing

We use Face Data solely to calculate aesthetic scores and provide personalized grooming recommendations. We do NOT use this data for identification, advertising, or training AI models.

03.

Third-Party Sharing

Photos are processed via OpenAI API (GPT-4o). OpenAI does not use data submitted via API to train their models. We never sell or rent your face data to any third parties.

04.

Storage & Security

Images are temporarily cached in a secure, encrypted Supabase bucket solely for transmission. All data is protected by industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption during transit.

05.

Data Retention Policy

CHADMAX AI does not retain face data. All uploaded photos are stored temporarily for a maximum of 60 seconds. This specific duration is required solely to facilitate the technical transmission and processing of the image to generate your analysis report. We do not store face data indefinitely; all information is permanently and automatically deleted from our servers immediately after the processing is complete. We do not maintain a database of user faces.

06.

User Rights & Control

Since we don't store photos permanently, there's no database to delete from. However, you can withdraw consent by not uploading photos. For inquiries, contact: chadmax.support@gmail.com.

07.

Compliance

This policy complies with App Store Guidelines 5.1.1/5.1.2, GDPR, and CCPA regarding the processing of biometric and personal data.
